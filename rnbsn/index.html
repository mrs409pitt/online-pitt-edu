Frequently Asked Questions

Do I ever need to visit the campus in Bradford? expand_more

No. This program is 100% online and designed specifically for remote learners. All coursework, including clinical projects, can be completed in your local community.

Can I work full-time while enrolled? expand_more

Absolutely. The majority of our students are full-time working nurses. The asynchronous format allows you to balance your professional responsibilities with your academic goals.

What are the tuition rates? expand_more

Pitt-Bradford offers highly competitive tuition rates for its online programs. We also provide financial aid counseling and support for employer reimbursement programs.

How long does the program actually take? expand_more

While students can finish in as few as two semesters of full-time study, we also offer a part-time track for those who need more time to balance work and life.
